Welcome to Norfolk Dog and Cat Hospital

Conveniently located in the heart of Norfolk, the Dog & Cat Hospital has been a staple of the Hampton Roads community for nearly 80 years. It is our mission to provide the highest quality of health care to your pets. With our devoted team of knowledgeable doctors, techs, and assistants, the Dog & Cat Hospital is committed to offering not only routine and preventative care for healthy animals, but also to relieve animal suffering with dignity and compassion in a benevolent and attentive environment. We understand that your pet is a cherished member of your family, and are devoted to providing excellent veterinary care for your beloved pet!
